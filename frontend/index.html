Clinical reasoning — model interpretation
This system is a clinical decision support tool validated on 253 ICU patients at SIUT Karachi (AUROC 0.989, sensitivity 98.4%, specificity 95.2% at decision threshold 47.7%). It does not replace clinical judgment. Feature attributions represent statistical contributions to the model's prediction, not causal clinical mechanisms. All predictions must be interpreted in the full clinical context by a qualified clinician.
